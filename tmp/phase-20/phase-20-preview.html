Additional States

My Page loading/error/missing profile

LoadingState/ErrorState/empty profile interests are represented through shared primitives. Missing nickname fallback is limited to my-page summary mapping.

Logout confirmation

로그아웃할까요?

cancel/confirm callbacks preserved. Processing disables both actions; errorMessage is displayed.

Account deletion normal/error/processing

Destructive variant, irreversible-risk copy, callback wiring, processing disable, and errorMessage display are covered.

Viewport Notes

360px

Grid chips wrap within the content sheet; bottom padding prevents save CTA and final settings rows from sitting under bottom navigation.

430px

Cards and policy text keep responsive width with readable line length and no fixed 393px absolute positioning.

Desktop width

Content remains centered in the production shell; policy text remains scrollable/readable and dialogs stay max-width constrained.
